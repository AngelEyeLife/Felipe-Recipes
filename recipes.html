Tomato Ragout

Ingridients


  • 1 Kilo white onions
  • 2 Kilo cherry tomatoes
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 4 stems of rosemary
  • 4 stems of thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Process


  1. Peel the outer layers of the onion until only white, cut the onion in half then cut it in small squares.
  2. Clean the garlic cloves and cut it in fine slices, or use a mortar to crush it to small pieces.
  3. For the rosemary and thyme, clean the stems and only use the leaves from both of them since we wonâ€™t be blending the sauce.
  4. Heat a pan medium heat with olive oil and add the onions garlic and herbs together. Here you want your ingredients to cook and not burn, the onions should start releasing a little bit of liquid and changing to a darker color.
  5. At this point you add the cherry tomatoes to the pan without cutting them. Add some salt and pepper and mix everything.
  6. Lower the heat of the fire and this needs to cook slowly for around 3 to 4 hours, steering it once in a while to check that it does not burn.
  7. During this time, you will see the tomatoes will start to release water and also decompose and this will make a very thick sauce.
  8. The sauce will be done when you have some piece soft tomatoes left but they will be soft and also a little sweet from the onions. The more you cook it the thicker it gets.

Enjoy!

Pumpkin Soup

Ingridients

  • 1 Kilo white onions
  • 2 Butternut squash whole
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 4 stems of rosemary
  • 4 stems of thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Process


  1. Peel the outer layers of the onion until only white, cut the onion in half then cut it in small squares.
  2. Clean the garlic cloves and cut it in fine slices, or use a mortar to crush it to small pieces.
  3. For the rosemary and thyme, clean the stems and only use the leaves from both of them.
  4. Peel both the butternut squash, cut it in half separating the thicker part from the thinner. Cut the ends of both pieces, cut it in half once again in the other direction.
  5. One side is going to have the seeds, these need to be removed with a spoon also removing the hair like part. Then just cut it all in to equal size chunks.
  6. Heat a pot medium heat with olive oil and add the onions garlic and herbs together. Here you want your ingredients to cook and not burn, the onions should start releasing a little bit of liquid and changing to a darker color.
  7. Add the pumpkin to the pan, a little salt and peper, mix it and add water enough to cover all the ingredients.
  8. Raise the fire to the max until the water is boiling, then lower it so is simmering or making a little bit of bubbles, so the pumpkin and everything else can continue cooking.
  9. Check regularly to see when you are able to squash the pumpkin easily with a spoon inside the pot.
  10. When pumpkin is soft enough blend everything, adjust the flavor to your liking with salt and pepper if needed. Your soup is ready to eat.

Enjoy!
